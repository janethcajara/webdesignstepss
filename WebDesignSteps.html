Web Design Steps

•Determine the Intended Audience

The colors, images, font and layout should be tailored to preferences of your audience. The type of site content (reading level, amount of animation, etc.) should be appropriate for youyr chosen audience.

•Determine the Goals of the Site

Some common goals of wed sites are: to be informative, to create a personal home page, to publish a corporate web presence, and to do business in e-commerse.

•Determine the General Content and Create a Site Map

The site map is sometimes calld a Storysboard. Common organizational structures for Web sites are: hierarchical , linear, and random.

•Sketch a Tentative Page Layoute

Be consistent in your overall layout color, typefaces, logo location, navigation bar, etc. Plan your Home Page Layout to avoid scrolling, Plan your Content Page Layout to be consistent, with easy navigation.
